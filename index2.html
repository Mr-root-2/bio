About Me

Hello! I'm Pritam Das, a dedicated Information Security Consultant and Ethical Hacker with over 6 years of hands-on experience in vulnerability assessments, penetration testing, and security audits. Born and raised in Raiganj, West Bengal, I’ve built my career on a passion for securing web applications, REST APIs, and mobile platforms. Currently, I’m working at AKS IT Services PVT. Ltd. in Noida, helping clients across government, banking, financial, and healthcare sectors strengthen their security posture.

I hold a CEH v10 certification from EC-Council and a B.Tech in Information Technology from Techno India College of Technology. My expertise includes deep knowledge of OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities, advanced usage of tools such as Burp Suite, Acunetix, Metasploit, Nmap, and SQLmap, and a proven track record of guiding development teams to remediation. Always staying ahead of emerging threats, I continuously expand my skill set through research, bug bounties, and community collaboration.
